Adjacent practices

Cross-cutting capabilities that touch every sector.

Two specialist disciplines run across all of our sector work — climate & disaster risk and social development & safeguards. They are not separate practice areas; they are part of how we structure every engagement.

Climate & disaster risk

Hazard, vulnerability and risk assessments; climate adaptation strategies; integration of disaster risk into project design and feasibility.

Social development & safeguards

Resettlement and rehabilitation planning, gender mainstreaming, stakeholder engagement and compliance with multilateral safeguard frameworks (World Bank, ADB, JICA).
