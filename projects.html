Common threads

What ties this work together.

Each of these engagements shares three traits — capital intensity, multi-stakeholder governance and a long operational tail. That's the shape of problem we're built for.

Capital-intensive

Multi-billion-rupee capex with debt-equity structures sensitive to small assumption shifts.

Multi-party governance

Sovereign authorities, lenders, JV partners and operators that must align on the same set of facts.

Long operational tail

Decisions made today are locked in for 25–40 years of operations. The cost of being wrong is decades-long.
