Leadership

Founding partners.

Mohit Sinha

Managing Director

Mohit brings more than two decades of advisory experience across core and social infrastructure. He has led teams on major engagements across India and internationally and was previously Chief Executive of Feedback Infra and Business Unit Head at Rodic Consultants before founding the current practice.

Practice Lead

Anindya is a senior economist with around 25 years of experience. He has led work on highways, urban transport, logistics, sanitation, water resources and social infrastructure programmes funded by multilateral development banks. His engagements span India, Lao PDR, Angola, Ghana, Bangladesh and several Sub-Saharan African economies.

Sudheendra

Operations Lead

Specialist bench

Cross-cutting expertise.

Beyond the founding partners, Stratavera draws on a network of senior specialists in disciplines that span every sector we work in.

SN

Dr. Sreeja Nair

Climate & Disaster Management Specialist

Dr. Nair has 25+ years of experience in disaster risk management and climate change adaptation, with a working history that spans government, the United Nations system, non-profits and the private sector at both national and international levels. Her core expertise covers hazard, vulnerability and risk assessments and the design of climate adaptation plans, policies and strategies.

HI

Hasanul Islam

Social Development Specialist

Hasanul is a senior social development, gender and resettlement & rehabilitation specialist with over 30 years of experience managing social safeguards on large infrastructure programmes. He holds a Master's in Sociology and has worked extensively on ADB, World Bank and JICA-funded engagements across urban infrastructure, water and sanitation, roads and highways, metro rail, bridges, tourism and forestry.
