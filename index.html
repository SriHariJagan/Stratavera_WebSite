What sets us apart

A small practice, built for the way infrastructure deals actually get done.

Most advisory engagements get handed off across teams; ours don't. The same senior bench that scopes the problem stays with you through structuring, bidding and post-award support.

Domain depth

Recommendations grounded in years of hands-on practice across transport, logistics, real estate and digital infrastructure — not generalist playbooks reskinned for the sector.

Full lifecycle

From concept note through structuring, financial close and post-award compliance — one bench, one accountability line. No reset between phases.

Senior bench

Engagements are led by professionals with two to three decades in their field. Junior teams support, but the principal is in the room when it matters.

Outcomes that ship

Our team's prior advisory has unlocked projects worth billions in capex — financial models that withstood audit, bid prices that won, structures that closed.

Services

Five disciplines, one accountability line.

01

Feasibility & DPRs

Traffic and demand assessment, alignment studies, cost estimation and detailed project reports that hold up before lenders and approving authorities.

02

Project structuring & PPP advisory

Concession design, risk allocation, contractual frameworks and viability gap funding strategy for build-operate, hybrid annuity and other PPP modes.

03

Project management consultancy

Owner's engineer support, programme governance and independent oversight through construction and commissioning.

04

Financing strategy

Capital structure design, debt-equity sizing, lender engagement and grant-blending strategies for capital-intensive infrastructure assets.

05

Bid process management

Bidder qualification frameworks, RFQ/RFP drafting, evaluation methodology and end-to-end transaction running on behalf of authorities.
