Why Stratavera

What working here actually looks like.

Most of the team has come from larger consulting and infrastructure organisations and chose to be here for a specific reason: smaller engagements, deeper involvement, and accountability that doesn't get diluted across layers.

That shapes how we work. Junior team members are not running models in a corner; they're in client meetings from day one. Senior leaders are not chasing utilisation; they're in the substance of every engagement.

  • High-leverage roles — small teams mean every contributor's work shows up in the final answer
  • Senior mentorship — direct exposure to professionals with two to three decades of operating experience
  • Real-world outcomes — projects that are built, financed and operated, not stuck in a strategy deck
  • Cross-sector exposure — work spans transport, urban, logistics and real estate inside a single year
  • International deployments — selective opportunities to work on engagements in Southeast Asia and Africa
Who we hire

We look for a specific shape of person.

We don't hire purely on credentials. We hire on demonstrated ability to think clearly under uncertainty, work cleanly in numbers, and earn trust with senior counterparts.

Analysts & Associates

Recent graduates or 1–3 year professionals from engineering, economics, planning or management backgrounds. Comfort with financial modelling and analytical writing is essential.

Senior Consultants & Managers

5–10 years of advisory or operating experience in infrastructure or a related sector. Track record of leading discrete work-products end to end.

Specialist hires

Senior professionals in transport modelling, financial structuring, climate risk, social safeguards or specific sub-sectors who want to operate inside a senior-led team.
