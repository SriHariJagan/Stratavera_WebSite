Transport.

Roads, urban rail and intercity passenger systems are where most of the team has spent its career. The result is a working knowledge of how each mode actually behaves in the real world — its demand drivers, its cost build-ups and the contractual conditions that make or break it.

What we cover

Sub-sectors of focus.

Within transport, Stratavera engages across the following sub-sectors. The same delivery team typically handles all of them, with specialist support pulled in when needed.

  • Highways and expressways — greenfield corridors, brownfield upgrades, hybrid annuity and BOT structures
  • Metro rail and suburban rail — financial structuring, bid advisory, technology choice support
  • Bus rapid transit and intercity bus terminals — terminal feasibility, route economics, fare structures
  • Civil aviation — traffic studies, airport demand forecasts, support to greenfield airport bids
  • Inland waterways and coastal shipping — modal shift potential and route viability

Where we typically engage

Pre-investment

Demand forecasts, alignment studies, cost estimates and feasibility reports that inform whether a project should proceed at all.

Transaction

Concession structuring, RFP drafting, bid evaluation and lender's engineer mandates through financial close.

Delivery & operations

Owner's engineer support, operations readiness reviews and post-commissioning performance monitoring.
