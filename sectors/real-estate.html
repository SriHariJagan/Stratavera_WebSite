Real estate.

Real estate is the bridge between hard infrastructure and the way people live and work. Our work in this sector focuses on the engagements where the asset's value is unlocked by transit access, public-private structuring or master-planned thinking — not by simple market timing.

What we cover

Sub-sectors of focus.

  • Transit-oriented development — value capture, density modelling and integration with transit assets
  • Mixed-use master planning — large-format estates with residential, commercial and civic components
  • Township and city-extension projects — site selection, phasing and infrastructure sequencing
  • Real estate strategy — entry, growth and portfolio strategies for institutional players
  • Industrial and warehousing parks — feasibility, anchor strategy and tenant economics

How we add value

Connecting transit and value

Our transport bench means we can model how a metro station, BRT corridor or expressway interchange will actually shift land economics — not assume it.

Public-private structuring

Many of the most interesting real estate opportunities in India sit on public land or rely on planning consents. We can structure these transactions for both sides of the table.

Bankable feasibility

Demand build-ups, absorption modelling and pricing strategies that hold up before lenders, JV partners and IC reviews.

How we engage

Typical mandates in real estate.

Master plan & phasing

Land use programming, infrastructure sequencing and phased development plans for large estates.

Asset feasibility

Bottom-up demand, pricing and absorption studies for individual mixed-use or single-use developments.

Transaction structuring

JV structuring, public-private deal design and concession frameworks for land-led transactions.

Investor advisory

Portfolio strategy, asset selection and entry strategy for institutional capital.

Asset turnaround

Repositioning strategies for under-performing or stalled real estate projects.

TOD & value capture

Modelling the land-value uplift around transit assets and structuring how authorities can monetise it.
