Logistics.

Goods movement is where infrastructure is judged most ruthlessly — every minute and rupee shows up in someone's landed cost. Our logistics work focuses on assets and corridors where modal choice, dwell time and tariff design meaningfully change the trade equation.

What we cover

Sub-sectors of focus.

  • Ports and harbours — cargo demand assessment, terminal sizing, concessioning
  • Inland waterways and coastal shipping — viability of modal shift, route economics, terminal planning
  • Multimodal logistics parks — site assessment, master planning, operating model design
  • Warehousing and distribution centres — demand sizing, network design, occupier strategy
  • Dedicated freight corridors — feeder studies, terminal economics, corridor business plans

Where we typically engage

Demand-side analysis

Cargo flow studies, hinterland mapping, modal share modelling and bottom-up demand forecasts.

Supply-side planning

Asset sizing, master planning, capacity phasing and integration with road and rail networks.

Commercial structuring

Tariff design, concession framework, anchor user strategy and operations & maintenance contracts.
