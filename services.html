Five service lines, designed to plug into a project at any stage.

Most of our clients come to us at the inflection points of a project — when the next decision can't be reversed cheaply. These are the disciplines we bring to those moments.

01 — Strategy & planning

Feasibility studies & detailed project reports.

Before a rupee of capex is committed, the project needs a defensible answer on three questions: Is the demand real? Is the alignment buildable? Will the numbers work? Stratavera produces the underlying analysis that lenders, regulators and approving authorities are willing to sign against.

Typical work-products

  • Traffic and demand assessment with bottom-up forecast models
  • Alternatives analysis and corridor / site selection
  • Preliminary engineering and capital cost estimates
  • Operating cost build-ups and life-cycle analysis
  • Financial and economic viability tests
  • Detailed project reports compliant with sector authorities
02 — Transaction

Project structuring & PPP advisory.

The right structure isolates risk on the party best placed to manage it — and prices in everything else. We design concession agreements, hybrid annuity contracts, EPC frameworks and viability gap funding mechanisms that reflect what the project actually is, not a template.

Where we typically help

  • Choice of delivery mode (BOT, HAM, EPC, BOOT, etc.)
  • Risk allocation matrices and contractual remedies
  • Concession agreement drafting and negotiation support
  • Viability gap funding structuring
  • Stakeholder mapping and consultation strategy
  • Authority capacity-building during transaction
03 — Delivery

Project management consultancy.

Once construction starts, the cost of slipped schedules and quality issues compounds quickly. We act as owner's engineer, programme governance lead or independent reviewer — depending on what the project sponsor needs to keep delivery honest.

Engagement formats

  • Owner's engineer and lender's independent engineer roles
  • Programme governance and milestone reviews
  • Contract administration and variation management
  • Quality assurance and design review
  • Commissioning and handover support
  • Post-construction operations readiness
04 — Capital

Financing strategy.

Infrastructure assets have to clear two separate bars: a bankable cash-flow story and a capital structure that the right pool of lenders is willing to fund. We design the second so that the first holds together over the asset's life.

What we cover

  • Capital structure design — debt / equity / mezzanine sizing
  • Lender engagement, term sheet negotiation and information memoranda
  • Multilateral and bilateral funding strategy
  • Grant blending and concessional finance integration
  • Refinancing and portfolio-level recapitalisation strategies
  • Investor information packs and roadshow support
05 — Award

Bid process management.

For authorities, a clean transaction depends on a defensible bidder qualification framework, an evaluation methodology that holds under challenge, and discipline through the run. For developers, it depends on a sharp price built from the asset up. We work both sides — separately, never together.

For sell-side authorities

  • RFQ and RFP drafting
  • Bidder qualification and evaluation methodology
  • Pre-bid clarification and bidder management
  • Bid evaluation and award support

For buy-side bidders

  • Bottom-up capex and opex assessment
  • Detailed financial modelling and bid pricing
  • Risk review and mitigation strategy
How we engage

Three engagement models. The right one depends on your project, not ours.

Lead advisor

Stratavera owns a defined work product end to end — DPR, transaction, model — and is fully accountable for the outcome.

Embedded support

Our specialists are seconded into your team for a defined window — useful when capacity is the bottleneck, not capability.

Independent reviewer

A second-opinion review of work done by other consultants where lender or board sign-off requires an outside view.
